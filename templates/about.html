Your Document Control Hub

This manufacturing-grade Document Management System is built for multi-plant operations. Everything from uploading a file to getting it approved, archived, and audited happens here.

How It Works - Quick Tour

Follow these steps to get started as a new user.

1
Log in with your credentials

Use your company email and password. First-time users can register from the login page. Your plant and department are set by your admin and auto-attach to every document you upload.

2
Upload a document

Go to Upload Documents in the sidebar. Drop a PDF, Word, Excel, or PowerPoint file. Select the customer (or mark it Internal). Hit Upload - the file is saved and an approval request email is sent automatically to the approver.

3
Wait for approval

Your document lands in Pending items. The admin approver receives an email with a review link, signs in, then approves or rejects the request. You get a notification email once a decision is made.

4
Browse the Document Library

All controlled documents live in the Document Library. Switch between tabs - Procedures, Std. Manual, CQ Manuals, Core Tool Manuals, Business Procedures, Awards, and Certifications. Each tab has its own browsing experience.

5
Browse Master Records & Customer Records

Master Records organizes documents by plant to department. Customer Records organizes them by customer and shows all matching files in one view. Both are read-only browsing views.

6
Track revisions

Every time a document is updated (Admin only), a new version is created and stored. The old version is never deleted. Visit Revision History to see the full version trail for any document.

7
Admin tools

Admins can delete documents (moved to Archive), restore archived files, view the full System Log (every login, upload, view, delete action), and update documents to create new versions.

Visual charts showing document counts by status (Approved / Pending / Rejected) across plants and departments.

User Roles

What each role can do.

  • Admin - full access: upload, approve or reject requests, delete, update versions, view system log, manage archive
  • Manager / Approver - upload and view documents
  • User - upload and view documents; no delete or admin tools
